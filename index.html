The problem

Two groups of people.
Never in the same room.

Founders need creative co-founders. Creatives want to build something meaningful. The problem? There's no structured way for them to find each other.

01

Founders can't find creative talent they trust

Hiring freelancers is transactional. Agency work is expensive. What founders actually need is a creative partner who's invested in the outcome — not just the invoice.

02

Creatives want more than client work

Senior creatives are tired of executing briefs for brands they don't care about. They want to build something. But finding the right founder to build it with is nearly impossible.

03

Networking events don't create real connections

You show up, exchange cards, never follow up. The format is broken. What actually creates trust is working alongside someone — even for just a few hours.

How it works

Simple by design.
Powerful by outcome.

Four steps. One monthly session. The rest is up to you and your group.

01

Sign up and fill out your brief

Tell us who you are, what you're building or working on, and what you're looking for in a collaborator. Takes 5 minutes. This is what we use to match you.

02

Get matched into a balanced group

Once a month, we match founders and creative professionals into equal groups based on your briefs. Always 50/50 — the goal is genuine compatibility, not just availability.

03

Meet in person for a co-working session

Your group meets for a structured in-person co-working session in Berlin. Work together, share perspectives, and see if the chemistry is there.

04

Build from there

Some matches lead to collaborations. Some lead to a co-founder relationship. Some lead to nothing — and that's fine too. New month, new match.
