What We Do

Four pillars.
One goal: traction before launch.

Makers Klub is a pre-launch growth agency for startup founders and entrepreneurs. We help you build audience, authority, and momentum before you go live — through camera confidence, content strategy, product validation, and community engagement.

01

Founder Camera Confidence

We help founders show up on camera naturally and confidently — so they can connect with their audience personally and build real trust before the product exists.

02

Content & Storytelling Strategy

We build a content strategy and narrative around your brand and product — so the right people understand what you are building, why it matters, and why you are the one to build it.

03

Product Validation

We help you use content and community to engage your target audience early — gathering real feedback that shapes the product and proves demand before you invest further.

04

Audience & Community Building

We help you spread the word, grow a genuine audience, and build a community around your brand — so launch day is a celebration, not a cold start.

About the Founder

Hi, I am Tushar.
Founder of Makers Klub.

A few years ago, I was told I was a failure. I felt invisible — professionally and personally. Getting in front of a camera, awkward and uncertain as I was, changed my life.

"Not overnight. But slowly, showing up on camera gave me back my voice, my confidence, and eventually my career."

I went from 17 to 300 YouTube subscribers in a year. That sounds small. But for someone who had been told they had nothing worth saying, it felt like everything.

I built Makers Klub because I know what it takes to go from invisible to seen — and I want to help founders do the same thing, faster, with the right support around them.

Tushar Rungta
Founder, Makers Klub · Camera Coach · Berlin
