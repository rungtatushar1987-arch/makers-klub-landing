What it is

The room
you've been
looking for.

"Both sides of the table — in the same room. That's where the interesting things happen."

Makers Klub is a members-only community in Berlin for creative freelancers and the business owners who want to work with them. We run regular events — socials, co-working days, and curated lunches — designed to close the gap between two groups who need each other but rarely find each other well.

This is not a networking platform. There are no algorithms, no cold DMs, no performance. Just a well-curated room, a good coffee, and people who are serious about what they do.

How we run things

Small, intentional,
worth attending.

🎯

Quality over quantity

We're not trying to scale to thousands of members. Every event should feel considered. Every introduction should feel worth making. The value is in the curation, not the numbers.

🤝

Both sides of the table

A community of only creatives is a support group. A community of only clients is a pitch fest. Makers Klub works because it holds both — and keeps the ratio honest.

🏙️

Real work, not performance

No panels, no thought leadership, no building-in-public theatre. Members show up with actual problems and leave with actual leads. Berlin-rooted, internationally minded.
