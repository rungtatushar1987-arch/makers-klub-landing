A monthly co-working platform for founders and creative professionals.

Every month, we match you with the right people based on what you're building — and bring you together for a focused, in-person co-working session in Berlin. No cold outreach. No random networking. Just the right people in the room.

You fill a brief. We handle the matching. You just show up.

The Problem

Working alone is the default.
It shouldn't be.

01
Co-working spaces feel isolating

You're surrounded by people but connected to none of them. Nobody knows what you're building. Nobody can help.

02
Networking events go nowhere

You collect business cards, exchange pleasantries, and follow up with people you'll never actually work with.

03
The right people are hard to find

Finding a designer who gets your vision, or a founder worth backing, shouldn't require scrolling LinkedIn for hours.

The Solution

We put the right people
in the same room.

Makers Klub matches founders and creative professionals based on what they're actively building — then brings them together for monthly in-person co-working sessions.

No forced networking. No awkward intros. Just focused people doing real work, with the option to help each other along the way.

✦ Matched by brief ✦ In-person sessions ✦ Vetted community ✦ Berlin-based

How It Works
