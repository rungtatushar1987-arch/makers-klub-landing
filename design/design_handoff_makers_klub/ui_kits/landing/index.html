How it works
The problem

The way it
usually works
isn't working

Founders need creative co-founders. Creatives want to build something meaningful. The problem? There's no structured way for them to find each other.

Networking events go nowhere.

You collect business cards you'll never follow up on. The conversations are surface-level, the energy is forced, and you leave wondering why you came. Connections made in a rush rarely go anywhere.

The right people are hard to find.

Not for lack of talent in this city, but talent and fit aren't the same thing. Finding someone who gets what you're building, and who you actually want to work with, takes more than a LinkedIn search.

Co-working spaces feel isolating.

You're surrounded by talented people, but everyone has their headphones on. You share a building, not a purpose. Days pass without a single meaningful exchange.

There's a better way to meet

Founders and Creatives find each other in 4 Steps

Makers Klub matches founders and creative professionals based on what they're actively building, then brings them together for monthly in-person co-working sessions across Berlin.

01

Sign up

Tell us who you are, what you're building or working on, and what you're looking for in a collaborator. Takes 5 minutes. This is what we use to match you.

02

Get matched

Once a month, we match founders and creative professionals into equal groups based on your briefs. The goal is genuine compatibility, not just availability.

03

Meet in person

Your group meets for a structured in-person co-working session in Berlin. Work together, share perspectives, and see if the chemistry is there.

04

Build from there

Some matches lead to collaborations. Some lead to nothing, and that's fine too. New month, new match.
